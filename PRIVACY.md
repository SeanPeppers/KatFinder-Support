# Privacy Policy for KatFinder
**Last Updated:** April 27, 2026

**KatFinder** ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to providing a fun, productive tab management experience while respecting your absolute right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle data.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
KatFinder is designed with a **Privacy-First** architecture.

* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or browsing history to any external servers.
* **Local Processing:** All logic used to determine your **"Cat Mood"** (tab counting) and **"Heatmapping"** (last-accessed timestamps) happens entirely on your local machine.

## 2. Permissions and Why We Need Them
To provide the core features of KatFinder, the extension requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| `tabs` | Used to count open tabs to update the Cat’s mood and identify "stale" or duplicate tabs for "Heatmapping" and "Pounce" features. |
| `storage` | Used to save earned **"Fish Treats,"** unlocked Shop items, and UI preferences. This data stays on your device. |
| `alarms` | Used to periodically check for stale tabs in the background. |

## 3. Data Storage
All data generated by KatFinder, including your game progress and shop unlocks, is stored using the `chrome.storage.local` API. 
> **Note:** This data is stored locally on your computer and is automatically deleted if you uninstall the extension.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We **never** sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. There is no background tracking, no analytics pings, and no remote advertisements.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our features or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted here on GitHub.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the code behind KatFinder, please **open an issue** in this GitHub repository.
